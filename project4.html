Online Resume Builder

The “Online Resume Builder” is a web-based application that designed for simplifying and enhancing the process of creating the professional resumes. In today’s job market, a well-crafted resume is needed for securing the job opportunities. The main aim is to empower the job seekers and providing the tools and necessary guidance for creating the fulfilling resume.The resume builder guides users through a structured process for collecting and organizing information required for their resumes ensuring they cover essential details. The student and professional sections distinguish between the needs of students and working professionals. In this application ReactJS is used as front end and MongoDB for backend development. In future offering benefits to the users like posting their resumes in job portals, suggestions for jobs, free of cost increases the chance of success in the competitive job market and functionalities will be added.
