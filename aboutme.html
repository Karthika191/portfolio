About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Applications (MCA) at Kongu Engineering College. I have a passion for creating websites and have developed skills in front-end development, back-end programming, and web design. My academic journey has been marked by consistent excellence, with achievements in various subjects and projects.

Outside academics, I actively participate in extracurricular activities. I have been involved in clubs like the Rotract Club, where I contributed to organizing events and workshops. These activities have enhanced my teamwork and leadership skills, crucial for both personal growth and professional development.

One of my notable achievements includes presenting a research paper titled "Whatsapp groupchat analysis by using Machine learning" at an international conference. This experience not only allowed me to showcase my research and communication abilities but also provided me with valuable insights into current trends and innovations in the field of computer science.
